Chicken Stirfry

What is a chicken Stirfry?

AStirfry is chicken breast that has been cut up into small pieces and cooked on a pan or pot with a bunch of veggies

Ingredients

  • chicken breast
  • onion
  • green BP
  • yellow BP
  • red BP
  • minced garlic
  • salt
  • goya all purpose

Steps

  1. cut up the chicken
  2. slice the veggies
  3. heat up pan and oil
  4. throw in you chicken and flip till is all evenly white
  5. let the water evaporate till oil comes out
  6. add all spices and one after the other, flip after adding each spice
  7. finally add all veggies at once
  8. cook until you arrive at the prefered softness of your veggies
